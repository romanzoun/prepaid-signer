# Pricing & Terms

No packages. No subscription. **Charged per signature.**

## Price per signature (incl. 8.1% VAT)

| Signature level | Price per signing person |
| --- | --- |
| QES (qualified) | CHF 3.40 |
| AES (advanced) | CHF 1.90 |
| SIMPLE | CHF 1.20 |

## Key rules

- Every applied signature is charged based on selected level.
- There are **no package prices**.
- Total price scales linearly with signer count.

## Included

- Signing flow with selected level (QES/AES/SIMPLE)
- Invitation delivery to signers
- Process traceability
- Signed PDF for participants

## Payment methods

Payment via **Stripe**:

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay

**No TWINT.**

## Refund policy

There are **no refunds**.
