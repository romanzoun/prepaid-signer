# FAQ

## Do I need an account?

No. justSign works without registration.

## What file type is supported?

PDF only.

## How is pricing calculated?

Per applied signature:

- QES: CHF 3.40
- AES: CHF 1.90
- SIMPLE: CHF 1.20

## Are there package prices?

No.

## Which payment methods are supported?

Credit card, Apple Pay and Google Pay via Stripe.
No TWINT.

## Is there a refund?

No. There are no refunds.
