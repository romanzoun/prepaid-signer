# How justSign works

justSign is a prepaid digital signing flow without subscription and without package pricing.

## Flow

1. Upload PDF
2. Add signers
3. Place signature fields
4. Choose signature level (QES, AES, SIMPLE)
5. Pay once
6. Invitations are sent automatically

## Price model

No packages. Every applied signature is charged per signing person:

- QES: CHF 3.40
- AES: CHF 1.90
- SIMPLE: CHF 1.20

(all incl. VAT)

## Payment

Payments run via Stripe.
Accepted methods: credit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay.

No TWINT.
