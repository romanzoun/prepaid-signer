# Getting started - first digital signature in 5 minutes

## Requirements

- PDF document (max. 20 MB)
- Signer contact details
- Payment method: credit card, Apple Pay or Google Pay

No TWINT.

## Steps

1. Open [Sign page](/sign)
2. Upload PDF
3. Add signers
4. Place signature fields
5. Choose level (QES/AES/SIMPLE)
6. Review price and pay

## Price per signature (incl. VAT)

- QES: CHF 3.40
- AES: CHF 1.90
- SIMPLE: CHF 1.20

## Note

No package prices and no refunds.
