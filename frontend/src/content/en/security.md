# Security at justSign

Security and privacy are core requirements at justSign.

## Safeguards

- TLS-protected transport
- Encrypted temporary document storage
- Server-side session management

## Signing & payment

- Signature technology via Swisscom Sign
- Payment via Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1)
- No direct access to raw card data by justSign

## Compliance

- GDPR aligned
- nFADP aligned
- eIDAS/ZertES-oriented workflows
